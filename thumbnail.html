WHAT'S A THUMBNAIL?

A thumbnail is a small, eye-catching image that represents a video. It plays a crucial role in boosting engagement by drawing viewers' attention and encouraging them to click on your video. A well-designed thumbnail provides a visual preview of the content, helping viewers quickly understand what the video is about and whether it interests them. By increasing the click-through rate (CTR), compelling thumbnails can lead to more views and higher visibility for your videos, ultimately contributing to the growth and success of your channel.
