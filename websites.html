WHAT'S A WEBSITE?

A website is a collection of web pages accessible online through a specific URL. These pages can include text, images, videos, and more, serving various purposes. Common types of websites include e-commerce sites like Amazon, where users can shop online; blogs with regularly updated content; social media platforms like Facebook for user interaction; news websites like BBC for current events; and portfolio sites showcasing personal or professional work, often used by artists or freelancers.
